Jeff Bezos

Jeffrey Preston Bezos (born January 12, 1964) is an American business magnate, media proprietor, and investor.

Bezos is the founder and executive chairman of Amazon, having previously served as chairman, president and CEO of the company.

With a net worth of more than $200 billion as of June 2021, he is the richest person in the world according to both Forbes and Bloomberg's Billionaires Index.
