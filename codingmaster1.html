Elon Musk

Elon Reeve Musk FRS (born June 28, 1971) is an entrepreneur and business magnate.

He is the founder, CEO, and Chief Engineer at spaceX early stage investor,CEO , and Product Architect of Tesla, founder of The Boring Company, and co-founder of Neuralink and OpenAI.

A centibillionaire, Musk is one of the richest people in the world.
